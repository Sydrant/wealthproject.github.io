What is poverty?

Poverty means barely having enough resources to meet the basic needs.

Why fighting poverty is important?

By fighting poverty, we can make millions of lives a lot easier and by doing that they can help others.

How to fight poverty?

By simply donating money or things that you don't need to organizations that help fight poverty.
